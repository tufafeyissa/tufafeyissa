About Me

Hi, I'm Alakesh Kalita, currently pursuing PhD (from January 2018) with the department of Computer Science and Engineering in Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, India, under the supervision of Dr. Manas Khatua.

It's been more than 4 years, I'm working in the field of Internet of Things. Back in 2016, I worked in an Indian government sponsored project titlled "Quality of Service Provisioning in Internet of Things" as a research scientist. Later, in 2017, I worked in Internet of Vehicle in IIIT Guwahati. Currently, I'm working in Industrial Internet of Things. The main topic of my research is "Adaptive resource allocation for faster formation of 6TiSCH network."

My research interests include Internet of Things, Cloud computing, Software Defined Network, Blockchain
